Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

SyntheticArchive Publisher is a local desktop/web content workflow tool used by the app owner to prepare, schedule, and upload approved visual content to connected social media accounts.

The app may store local configuration data needed to operate the workflow, including platform authorization tokens, account identifiers, media file paths, captions, scheduling information, and publishing status.

This data is stored locally on the app owner’s computer and is not sold, shared, or transferred to advertisers or third parties.

When the app connects to TikTok, it may request access to basic account information and video upload permission. This access is used only to confirm the connected creator account and upload approved content to TikTok drafts for final review.

SyntheticArchive Publisher does not collect public user data, does not operate a public user database, and does not allow unrelated third-party users to access the app.

The app owner may revoke platform access at any time through the connected platform’s account or app authorization settings.

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the app owner directly.
